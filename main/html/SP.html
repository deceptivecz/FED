My Experience in Singapore Polytechnic

Special Interest Groups (SiGs)

I am currently in 2 Special Interest Groups: Gryphons and SOCA.

DISM Gryphons is an SIG run by DISM students, the team runs workshops
and competitions such as the annual Gryphons CTF competition.

SOCA is an SIG run by SOC students, the team aims to provide outstanding SOC
students with avenues and pathways to excel as a leader in SOCA, SOCA runs
workshops and executes outreach events to secondary school students.
