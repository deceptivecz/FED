Cyber Security Analyst
 
S$27k - S$93k

The role of a cybersecurity is to protect company property and networks from cybercriminals. The analysts primary focus is to understand a company's IT infrastructure and evaluate threats that could potentially breach the network.

CyberNSF Scheme

In 2018, MINDEF officially announced that a new section within the mandary 2-year National Service would be opened. This new section was called the NSF Cyber Scheme. All NS pre-enlistees are eligible to apply for this post, and select NSFs who possess the aptitude will be selected. I hope to join the CyberNSF Scheme during my National Service and potentially even sign-on in the Cyber Vocation as a Cyber Operator.

Cyber Security Agency of Singapore (CSA)

When I first started studying information security and playing CTFs, CSA would consistently make an appearnce in all of my events. CSA is a highly reputed cybersecurity organisation in Singapore and their core mission is to protect Singapore's cyberspace.



I hope to join CSA as a cybersecurity analyst for an internship or to join as a full-time.
