Cyber Security Analyst
 
S$27k - S$93k

The role of a cybersecurity is to protect company property and networks from cybercriminals. The analysts primary focus is to understand a company's IT infrastructure and evaluate threats that could potentially breach the network.

CyberNSF Scheme

In 2018, MINDEF officially announced that a new section within the mandary 2-year National Service would be opened. This new section was called the NSF Cyber Scheme. All NS pre-enlistees are eligible to apply for this post, and select NSFs who possess the aptitude will be selected. Vocational training will be provided to selected NSmen. Additionally, work-study programs are provided by renowned local universities to upgrade the skills of our Cyber NSmen.
